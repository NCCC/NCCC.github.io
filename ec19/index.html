Easter Camp 2019

19th - 22nd April 2019

About

What is Easter camp?

A place where you can have fun and experience God's love. This camp is a place where you can be part of a christian fellowship.

During these days there will be games, activities, discussions, sharings and so much more! Whether christian or not, you are warmly welcome to come and have the opportunity to hear other people's experiences with Christianity.

Do you want to know how normal people just like you and me came across Christianity and how it's been impacting their lives? Join us to find out!

Who can join?

We welcome everyone to join the camp, so bring your friends as well if they are interested!

The content of the camp primarily targets people in the age of 14-28 years old, however more mature people are also warmly encouraged to join us.

Good to be know that our main language during the camp will be English.

How do I join?

In order to register, simply navigate to Registration section, fill in the registration form and pay the camp fee accordingly. Please notice that only registrations with received payment are regarded as valid.

More information regarding the transportation and payments can be found in the Registration section. After registering you will receive an email with more detailed information.

Registration

How much is it?

Early Birds: 500NOK / 500SEK / 50EUR
Others: 600NOK / 600SEK / 60EUR

Want to get the Early Bird discount? Then register and pay the camp fee by the 1st of March!

In addition you will need some money for public transportation between the campsite and Stockholm Central, and arrange transportation from your home to Stockholm Central.

As for the payments, we will recommend you to use one of the following:

    Sweden, Swish
    Norway, Vipps
    Finland, MobilePay

More detailed payment information will be given to you after filling the registration form.

How do I get there?

Arrive Stockholm C by 16:15 Fri 19th
Depart campsite after 12:00 Mon 22nd

Arrival

    Take the public transportation departuring 14:46 or 16:46 from Stockholm Central. We will meet you at the station!
    Alternatively you may drive to campsite (Drakudden Läger & Kursgård, Smidövägen 17, 197 91 BRO), please arrive by 18:00 for dinner.

Departure
    Take the public transportation from the campsite, more info will be given later once timetables are confirmed.
    Alternatively drive back home after 12:00 when camp ends.

What should I bring?

Here is a checklist for you!

    Notebook, pencil/pen
    Some money for public transportation
    Pillow case, bed sheet and quilt cover
    Toiletries (toothbrush, paste, towels, shampoo, etc.)
    Clothes for different weather
    Slippers (the floors are cold)
    Bible (physical or electronic)
    Snacks if you like to snack :)
