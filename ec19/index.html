About

What is Easter camp?

Four days of exploring the controversial topics, how christianity covers them and making friends along the way. There will be lectures, singing, games and discussions.

Whether you are a christian or not, you might have come across with questions about christianity in which you have never gotten a proper answer to or dared to ask. We hope to cover some of these tricky questions during the days. Come and join us!

Who can join?

We welcome everyone to join the camp so bring your friends too!

We aim to be welcoming to everyone who is interested! The agegroup is 13-25 years old, but you're welcome to join even if you are more mature ;) Also please notice that the main language during the camp is English.

How do I join?

In order to registrate, simply navigate to Registration section, fill in the registration form and pay the camp fee accordingly. Please notice that only the registration with received payment are regarded as valid registrations.

More information regarding the transportation and payments can be found in the Registration section. Do read the instructions carefully.

Registration

How much is it?

Early birds: 500NOK / 500SEK / 50EUR

After: 600NOK / 600SEK / 60EUR

In addition you will need 250 SEK for transportation from Stockholm C to the camp site, and arrange transportation from your home to Stockholm C

You can pay the camp fee by...

  • Vipps (Norway)
  • Swish (Sweden)
  • MobilePay (Finland)

The payment information will be given to you after filling the registration form.

How do I get there?

Arrive Stockholm C by 17:00 Fri 19th
Depart campsite after 12:15 Mon 22nd

Arrival: Take the bus Gbg C - Ljungskile station 14:21 - 15:11 or 16:21 - 17:11. We will meet you at the Ljungskile station!
Or drive to campsite (Restenäs 239, 459 93 Ljungskile) and arrive between 14:00 - 18:00.

Departure: Take the bus Campsite - Gbg C 14:02-15:43 (35 min transfer in Ljungskile).
Or drive back home after 12:15 when camp ends.

What should I bring?

  • Notebook, pencil/pen
  • 250 SEK for Sthlm C-campsite-Sthlm C
  • Pillow case, bed sheet and quilt cover
  • Toiletry (toothbrush, paste, towels, shampoo, etc.)
  • Clothes for different weather
  • Slippers (the floors are cold)
  • Bible (physical or electronic)
