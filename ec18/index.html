About

What is Easter camp?

Four days of exploring the controversial topics, how christianity covers them and making friends along the way. There will be lectures, singing, games and discussions.

Whether you are a christian or not, you might have come across with questions about christianity in which you have never gotten a proper answer to or dared to ask. We hope to cover some of these tricky questions during the days. Come and join us!

Who can join?

We welcome everyone to join the camp and bring your friends too!

We aim to be welcoming to everyone who is interested! The agegroup is 13-25 years old, but you're welcome to join even if you are more mature ;) Also please notice that the main language during the camp is English

How do I join?

In order to registrate, simply navigate to Registration section, fill in the registration form and pay the camp fee accordingly. Please notice that only the registration with received payment are regarded as valid registrations.

More information regarding the transportation and payments can be found in the Registration section. Do read the instructions carefully.
